# About Us

Our lab’s interdisciplinary research utilizes a combination of computational systems biology and machine learning tools, as well as molecular biology/cell biology/"-omics" based experimental techniques to deeply characterize the molecular processes of mammalian and microbial cells to develop innovative and industry-ready solutions for the manufacturing of biopharmaceuticals, cultivated meat and cell therapy products. We also develop computational tools to analyze large-scale multi-omics data and build mechanism based computational models, with a particular focus to enhance human health and well-being aspects. Our major research areas include systems biological analysis of cellular metabolism, protein homeostasis, transcriptional regulation, cell differentiation, immunometabolism and their interconnection with microbiomes.
